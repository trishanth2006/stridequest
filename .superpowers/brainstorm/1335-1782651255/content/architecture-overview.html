Live Run Notification

Timer fires every 1s inside the hook. Notification updates only every 5s — throttled in NotificationManager to avoid JS bridge spam.

Territory Capture Anti-Spam

Captures within a 60s window are batched. If you grab 5 zones in 2 min, you get one "5 territories captured!" buzz, not 5.
